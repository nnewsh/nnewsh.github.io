Dr Niall Newsham

Hi, I am Dr Niall Newsham, a postdoc at the MOVE research lab within UC Santa Barbara. I am a population geographer interested in understanding contemporary demographic change between places and across space. My research intersects demography and geography, exploring themes such as population decline, low fertility, migration flows, and mobility trajectories using advanced quantitative methods. I hold a Ph.D. in Human Geography and an M.Sc. in Demography and Population Studies from the University of Liverpool, UK.
