Dr Niall Newsham

Hi, I am Dr Niall Newsham, a postdoc at the MOVE research lab within UC Santa Barbara. I am population geographer interested in understanding contemporary demographic change across space and between places. My research intersects demography and geography, and explores themes such as population decline, low fertility, migration flows and mobility trajectories. I hold a Ph.D. in Human Geography, and an M.Sc. in Demography and Population Studies from the University of Liverpool, UK.
